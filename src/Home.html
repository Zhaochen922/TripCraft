About TripCraft

The primary purpose of "TripCraft" is to simplify and personalize the travel planning process. By allowing users to input specific preferences such as destinations, budget constraints, and travel themes, "TripCraft" generates tailored travel suggestions that optimize the overall experience while considering factors like local attractions, accommodations, and transportation options.
